Ride Or Whaite

I asked the cards whether to ride or waite
I didn’t see anything new
just
a reflection
reversed, not mirrored but flipped.
the hanged man.
a symbol of stagnation. of standstill

The position tells me that this is my Perception
it may be true it may be false but it’s how I see myself
not stuck per se?
it could be a self imposed sacrifice
a necessary step to see the world directly

the other cards encouraged action and momentum
to just keep moving
is that what went wrong?
that I tried to look before I lept?
I’ve always struggled with commitment
and symbolism for that matter
maybe they knew that
but would that really change the message?

He let me keep the six of clubs
a very auspicious card
promising success and payoff after long hard work
that I was right to be optimistic
and
I lost it.
during the rest of my travels

all I had left was the 3 of Spades
foreshadowing heartbreak and a storm.
a storm did come, albeit not quite the one foretold by that card
and I whethered it as well as I could
but I’m still wary to go out in the rain

and this house is showing its age
the raindrops, rhythmic and relentless
have soaked their way through the roof
to spatter the reflection in front of me

The hanged man
is not Judgement
nor condemnation
he is where he needs to be
serene, contemplative
making use of unique perspective
while he can before he must act

the cards won’t Give me the answer
I know that’s not how it works
but I’m always going to want to ask

I dream of the day that I don’t
craving certainty, just as much as accomplishment

but that day isn’t here
and until then
I won’t know for sure
whether I needed to act
or stop seeing myself in the gallows
the cards aren’t meant to give answers
do I just have to settle for more questions?
or is there something I’m choosing not to see?
